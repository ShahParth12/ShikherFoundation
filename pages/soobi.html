DONATION

We want your support by gathering your unused items from your home and office which you will scrap not to ever use aagain. This may be scrap for you but can be new for someone in need.

This donation will help to fulfill the basic needs of the under privileged people and donations help sustain our organization. This will also benefit the community which we serve and together we can bring smile on a lot of people's faces.

Since our inception we have received donation from 1000+ donors.

  • Used Laptops, Computers & any electronic parts
  • Used Sewing Machine & any used electronic appliances
  • Used watter bottles, tiffin box, compass box, school bag/ officebag/ travel bag
  • Old clothes/ Uniforms
  • Any scrap
